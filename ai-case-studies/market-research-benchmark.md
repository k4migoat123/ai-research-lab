**Models tested:** Claude 3.7, ChatGPT GPT-4o, DeepSeek, Gemini

## Task Brief

Research the mobile auto detailing and glass installation market in Austin, TX. Identify top competitors, estimate market size, and recommend a pricing strategy.

## Key Research Questions

1. What is the approximate TAM for mobile detailing in Austin?
2. Who are the top 3-5 competitors and what do they charge?
3. What pricing tier is optimal for a new entrant?
